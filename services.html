What is Semi-Permanent Make- Up?

Semi-permanent Make-up is a complexion treatment to enhance the face features to add definition to the face. This technique involves injecting pigment through a fine vibrating needle into the superficial layers of the skin.

Ombre Powdered Brows $475. This style is for clients who love their brows filled in with make up daily for an everyday look while the inner brow is softer and lighter and darker from mid to the end of the tail. This style does not have any hair like Microblade strokes added. Color corrections or new clients with prior work elsewhere; please contact us for a consultation prior to booking.

Microshading/Hybrid Brows $525. This style has some Microbladed hairlike strokes added to the inner brow while the rest of the brow is powder like. This style is powder brow dominant. Color corrections or new clients with prior work elsewhere; please contact us for a consultation prior to booking.

Microblade $425 or Microblade w/shading $475. This style is for clients who love a natural look daily. Add some light shading to add depth, balance and definition to the brows. Shading may be required for some clients to complete their look depending on their natural brow shape. This style is Microblade dominant with some light shading. Color corrections or new clients with prior work elsewhere. Please contact us for a consultation prior to booking.

Scalp Micropigmentation $500-$2,500. This procedure is done by using a tattoo machine to insert pigment to the scalp to mimic hair follicles. This procedure is for anyone who is experiencing a receding hair line or thinning hair to help their hair appear thicker, fuller. For men who's hairline is receding, a new hair line can be created for fuller appearance. Please contact us for a consultation prior to booking.

Hair Color Services $85/Hr. All hair color services include Olaplex treatment to help minimize damage during the chemical process. I offer the following techniques: Balayage Ombres, all over colors, Balayage Highlights, full and partial highlights, peekaboos, lowlights, toning etc. I average about 5 hours each session.
