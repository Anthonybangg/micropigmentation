All classes will include 1-2 live models where I demonstrate each technique/procedure from start to finish on each model. All students will receive a kit which includes everything needed for training (tattoo machine(s), practice tattoo skin etc.) All classes will cover proper sanitation, different techniques to create beautiful brows, brow shaping, before and aftercare procedures, the different apps used to photoshop and create social media content, tips on how to start your brow career and a certificate of completion. You will also receive 1 year of support after your training and you are allowed to shadow me in person 1 time after your training up to 1 year after your training. You are required to take a bloodborne pathogens online training and bring your certificate of completion to your class. The cost is usually less than $100. I will send you a link to this training after you book your class. All brow training classes require a deposit of $300 to reserve your seat and the remainder will be due at the beginning of the class.
